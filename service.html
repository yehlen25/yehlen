My Services

  • Personalized Makeup Consultations

    Yehlen Rose specializes in providing personalized makeup consultations tailored to individual preferences, skin types, and occasions. Whether it's a casual day look, a glamorous evening event, or professional makeup for photoshoots, she ensures that her clients look and feel their best.

  • Skincare Guidance

    Understanding the importance of a healthy skincare routine, Yehlen Rose offers expert advice on skincare products and routines. She helps clients identify the right skincare products for their specific needs, addressing concerns and promoting a radiant complexion.

  • Color Matching and Product Recommendations

    With in-depth knowledge of Ever Bilena's extensive product line, Yehlen Rose assists clients in finding the perfect makeup products. From foundation and lipstick to eyeshadow and blush, she excels in color matching and recommends products that complement individual skin tones and preferences.

  • Makeup Application Workshops

    Yehlen Rose organizes makeup application workshops to empower individuals with the skills to enhance their own beauty. These hands-on sessions cover various techniques, from basic everyday looks to advanced styles, allowing participants to gain confidence in their makeup application skills.

  • Special Occasion Makeup

    For special events such as weddings, parties, or photoshoots, Yehlen Rose provides professional makeup services to ensure clients look flawless and camera-ready. Her attention to detail and creativity shine through in creating customized looks that align with the client's vision.

  • Beauty and Wellness Seminars

    Beyond makeup, Yehlen Rose conducts beauty and wellness seminars, focusing on topics such as self-care, confidence-building, and embracing one's natural beauty. These sessions aim to inspire individuals to prioritize their well-being and develop a positive relationship with themselves.
